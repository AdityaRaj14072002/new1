↓↓  Reipes of Egg Spaghetti(Scrambled Egg Pasta)   ↓↓

    INGRDIENTS:

  • Egg Spaghetti(Scrambled Egg Pasta)
  • water
  • Oil
  • Pasta
  • Garlic (Crushed)
  • Salt
  • Red Chili flakes
  • Souce
  • Egg

    INSTUCTIONS:

  1. Pour 10 cups of water to a large pot and bring it to a boil.
  2. Then add the pasta.
  3. Add some salt.Cook until all dente. Check the package for cook time.Set aside 4 to 5 tbsps of pasta coocked water.
  4. Drain the cooked pasta to a colander.
  5. While the pasta cooks, pour oil to a pan and heat up and add garlic.
  6. Saute until the garlic begins to shrink. Do not saute until the garlic browns
  7. When it turns aromatic, add red chili flakes.
  8. Saute for about 30 to 50 seconds. Then pour the pasta cooked water that we set aside. Cook untill the water evaporates and a slightly thick souce like mixture is left after it pour the eggs.
  9. Scramble them and cook until done. The eggs are fully cooked and set, yet soft. Check the taste and add more salt if needed.
  10. Add the drained pasta
  11. Toss well with the egg and add parsely.
  12. Serve egg pasta hot.
